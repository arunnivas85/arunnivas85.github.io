What We Do

To uphold in every way, the dignity and honor of Engineering profession.
To promote a sense of esprit de-corps among all Engineers.
To enable to take counsel together on matters relating and affecting to their profession.
To elevate engineering profession and to rise the standard of the profession.
To work in co-operation with others with interest and to promote integrity for our country’s progress.
To solve the unemployment problems of fellow engineers.
To promote the spirit of fraternity among the Engineers.
To promote the scope of keeping the members abreast of latest technique in Engineering field through the bulletins and periodicals.
